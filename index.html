iPad

iPad is a line of tablet computers designed, developed and marketed by Apple, Inc., which run the IOS mobile operating system. The first iPad was released on April 3, 2010; the most recent iPad models are ipad (2018), released on March 27, 2018, the 10,5-inch (270mm) and 12.9-inch (330mm) 2G iPad Pro released on June 13, 2017. The user interface is build around the device's multi-touch screen, including a virtual keyboard. All iPads can conncect via Wi-Fi, some models also have cellular connectivity.

iPhone X

iPhone X is a smartphone designed, developed, and marked by Apple, Inc. It was announced on September 12, 2017, alongside the iPhone 8 and iPhone 8 Plus at the Steve Jobst Theater in the Apple Park campus. The phone was released on November 3, 2017. This devise marks the iPhone series' tenth anniversiry, with "X" being symbol for "ten" in Roman numerals.

MacBook Air

The MacBook Air is a line of Macintosh subnotebook computers developed and manufactured by Apple, Inc. It consists of a full-size keyboard, a machined aluminum case, and a thin light structure. The Air is available with a screen size of (measured diagonally) 13.3in (33,782 cm), with different specifications produced by Apple. Since 2011, all MacBook Air models have used-state drive storage and intel Core i5 or i7 CPUs
